 🏥 Global Cancer Patient Analytics Dashboard

 Turning Cancer Data into Life-Saving Insights

 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Healthcare Analytics solution developed in Power BI using a dataset of 50,000 cancer patient records.

The dashboard analyzes patient demographics, cancer severity, treatment costs, survival outcomes, and key risk factors to support data-driven healthcare decisions.

---

 🎯 Business Problem

Healthcare organizations need to understand:

* Which factors increase cancer severity?
* Which patient groups have better survival outcomes?
* How do treatment costs impact patient survival?
* What risk factors should be prioritized for intervention?

This dashboard provides actionable insights to improve patient outcomes and support healthcare planning.

---

 ⚠️ Page 2 – Risk Factor Analysis

Analyzes the relationship between:

* Smoking and Severity
* Alcohol Use and Severity
* Obesity and Severity
* Air Pollution and Severity
* Genetic Risk and Severity

Key Findings:

* Genetic Risk shows the strongest relationship with severity.
* Smoking and Air Pollution demonstrate positive associations with severity.
* Obesity and Alcohol Use show moderate impact.

---

 ❤️ Page 3 – Patient Outcomes

Focus Areas:

* Survival by Cancer Type
* Survival by Gender
* Survival Trend by Year
* Treatment Cost vs Survival

Objective:
Identify factors that contribute to better patient outcomes and longer survival.

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

✅ Early diagnosis is associated with higher survival rates.

✅ Genetic Risk is a major contributor to cancer severity.

✅ Lifestyle factors such as smoking and obesity influence patient outcomes.

✅ Treatment investment may positively impact survival.

✅ Data-driven healthcare strategies can improve patient care.
